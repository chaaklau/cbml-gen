About CBML

The Comic Book Markup Language (CBML) is a specialized markup scheme for digital annotation and analysis of comic books and graphic narratives.

Key Features: Panel annotation • Character tracking • Transition classification • Attentional framing • Speech balloons • Custom elements

Learn more: dcl.luddy.indiana.edu/cbml
